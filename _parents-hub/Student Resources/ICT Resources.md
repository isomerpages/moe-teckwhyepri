<h4>School Learning Space (SLS) Helplines</h4>
<p><strong>A) School Operating Hours</strong>
</p>
<p>During school operating hours, students should contact the <strong>School-Based Helpline</strong> for
SLS queries including all SLS access matters such as password reset and
unlocking of accounts, as well as queries about lesson assignments.</p>
<p>School-Based Helpline: 9788 8640</p>
<p>Mondays - Fridays: 8:00 am - 4:00 pm</p>
<p>Alternatively, you could also contact the School-Based Helpline with the
following form:</p>
<p><a href="https://go.gov.sg/twpshelpline" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/twpshelpline</a>
</p>
<p><strong>B) Non-School Operating Hours</strong>
</p>
<p>Students can only contact the <strong>SLS Helpdesk</strong> outside the
school operating hours.</p>
<p>SLS Helpdesk: 6702 6513</p>
<p>Mondays - Fridays: 4:00 pm - 9:00 pm
<br>Saturdays: 9:00 am - 9:00 pm
<br>*Closed on Sundays &amp; Public Holidays</p>
<p>Alternatively, students can email the SLS Helpdesk at <a href="mailto:helpdesk@sls.ufinity.com" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">helpdesk@sls.ufinity.com</a>.
When emailing the SLS Helpdesk, they should include their full name, name
of school and form class.</p>
